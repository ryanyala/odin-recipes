Tortillas

Ingredients

  • 45g Masa Harina
  • 2g Salt
  • 78g Water

Steps

The steps to making fresh corn tortillas is actually quite easy.

  1. Weigh out and add masa harina into a bowl.
  2. Add in the salt.
  3. Slowly add in water as you mix. Knead the dough for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Evenly portion out 3 tortilla balls from the dough.
  5. Flatten each ball with a tortilla press and then cook on a griddle.
  6. Enjoy!
