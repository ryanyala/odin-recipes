Chipotle Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Lime juice
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients (excluding chicken) in a bowl
  2. Add in chicken to marinade and massage marinade into chicken
  3. Let rest overnight.
  4. Cook in oven!.
  5. Enjoy!
